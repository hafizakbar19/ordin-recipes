Paratha

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Water
  • Ghee
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Make the dough of the wheat flour after adding a pinch of salt
  2. Wait for 15 minutes
  3. Take a small peice of the dough and flat it in round shape
  4. Add some gee on it and roll it
  5. Make it flat again in round shape and shallow fry in the pan
