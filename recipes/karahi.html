Chicken Karachi

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Oil/Ghee
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes
  • Yogurt
  • Herbs

Steps

  1. Shallow fri onions
  2. Add Totamotoes when onoin get light brown along with herbs
  3. Add yogurt and mix
  4. Add some water to make the chicken well tender
  5. Cook for 15 - 20 minutes
  6. Garnish with green chilli and coriander
